# Decision Memo: West Africa NEX-GDDP Regional Fast Watchlist

## Status

Warning-only watchlist. Not general fast pool.

## Policy ID

`AFR-WAF-FAST-WARNING`

## Model set

- `INM-CM5-0`
- `IPSL-CM6A-LR`
- `TaiESM1`

## Why this subset

Current West Africa evidence is concentrated on monsoon daily rainfall extremes.
These three models have most defensible positive signals in that evidence slice.

- `INM-CM5-0`: smaller discrepancies in extreme rainfall estimates.
- `IPSL-CM6A-LR`: good heavy-rainfall-frequency support.
- `TaiESM1`: good heavy-rainfall-frequency support.

## Why this is warning-only

- evidence is extremes-focused, not broad seasonality/onset support
- no good multi-process West Africa shortlist yet
- Guinea Coast versus Sahel differentiation still weak

## Models explicitly cautioned

- `MIROC6`
- `GFDL-ESM4`
- `FGOALS-f3-L`

## Intended use

- exploratory West Africa extremes screening
- not general seasonal climate pool
- not onset/cessation pool
- not final structural uncertainty envelope

## Evidence basis

- Klutse et al. 2021: West Africa monsoon extreme precipitation evaluation
